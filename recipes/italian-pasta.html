Classic Italian Pasta Salad

Description

This pasta salad with broccoli, carrots, tomatoes, bell peppers, cauliflower, onions and mushrooms can easily be customized with your favorite vegetables.

  • 8 ounces rotelle or spiral pasta, cooked and drained
  • 2 ½ cups assorted cut-up vegetables (broccoli, carrots, tomatoes, bell peppers, cauliflower, onions and mushrooms)
  • ½ cup cubed Cheddar or mozzarella cheese
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 cup Wish-Bone Italian Dressing

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except the dressing in large bowl.
  2. Add dressing; toss well.
  3. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
